title:          "The G-protein coupled receptor GPR34 promotes the homeostatic state of microglia and restrains the disease-associated microglial response in an AD model"
date:           2025-11-26 00:01:00 +0800
selected:       true
pub:            "bioRxiv"
pub_date:       "2025"
abstract: >-
  GPR34 maintains microglial homeostasis and limits disease-associated microglial activation in an Alzheimer's disease model. Loss of GPR34 shifts microglia toward inflammatory profiles, enhances glial pathology in 5xFAD mice without affecting amyloid plaque burden, and impairs myelin phagocytosis in human iPSC-derived microglia.
